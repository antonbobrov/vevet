Class SectionScrollProgress<StaticProps, ChangeableProps, CallbacksTypes>

SectionScrollProgress is a component that traces the scroll progress of a specified section. It provides the progress of entering the viewport, moving within it, and exiting it.

See examples https://antonbobrov.github.io/vevet-demo/section-scroll-progress/
